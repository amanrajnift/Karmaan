How this works?
Donate Clothes for Free and Get Rewarded - Join Our Mission Today
Our company offers a hassle-free and rewarding clothing donation program. Simply donate your gently used clothes, shoes, and accessories, and we'll handle the rest. We accept donations free of cost and provide donors with rewards as a thank you for their contribution. Join us in our mission to support the environment and community.
Schedule a Pickup
Sign up online or call us to schedule a free pickup at your doorstep.
Donate Clothes
Bag your gently used clothes, shoes, and accessories and place them outside on the scheduled pickup day.
Receive Rewards
Receive rewards via email or our app based on the weight of the items donated.
Impact the Community
Your donation helps support local charities and reduce textile waste in landfills.
